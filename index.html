Medical student, consultant CTO, and policy analyst

Efficiency in one form or another is a solution to many of the issues currently facing our world. This can occur on two fronts -- through technological innovation and adoption, as well as effective policy on organizationation and institutional fronts. It is my goal to intertwine these aspects in improving health outcomes and delivery systems.

Foreward

I was born in the great city of Chicago, IL and have resided within the area for my entire life. From a young age, I have always been captivated by the lure of technology and innovation, and learned to code my first computer program at the age of 9.

But it wasn't merely the thrill of learning new programming languages and software suites that piqued my interest, but rather the application of these skills to addressing real-world issues, the solutions to which may have ever-lasting consequences on the lives and experiences of everyday people.

It is my hope that, by sparking innovation within individuals, organizations, and institutions, in conjunction with effective policy-making, opportunity and a superior quality of life will become accessible to a greater number of people. That has been the essence of my life's work, and will continue to be for the years to come.
